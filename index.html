featured_album

plastic death

glass beach

The album plastic death is kind of hard to nail down genre-wise. It has some elements from psych rock, emo, thrash metal, and even more. What I found especially unique about this album is that every song has it's own unique soundscape but collectively the songs all fit under a general vibe in a way that's hard to describe. I'm a big fan of the track "cul-de-sac" which I feel really captures the feeling invoked from the album's awesome album art.

Favorite Track: "cul-de-sac"
