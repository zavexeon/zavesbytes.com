The album *plastic death* is kind of hard to nail down genre-wise. It has some elements from psych rock, emo, thrash metal, and beyond. What I found especially unique about this album is that every song has it's own individual soundscape but collectively the songs all fit under a general vibe in a way that's hard to describe. I'm particulary a fan of the track "cul-de-sac" which I feel really captures the feelings invoked from the album's awesome cover art.
